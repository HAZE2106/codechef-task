AZZURI BAY


The Azzuri Bay cafe in Chennai has an ideal rooftop setting for a romantic date. One can enjoy a wide variety of both south and north Indian dishes. They absolutely stand out because of their seafood. So please don't give it a miss!

LOCATION 13, 1st Crescent Road, Gandhinagar, Adyar
AVERAGE COST FOR TWO INR 1000
TIMINGS 11:30 AM- 11 PM
