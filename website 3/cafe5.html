CAFE DE PARIS



The aesthetics of Cafe de Paris are far from ordinary. This place has quite a warm, comforting and delightful ambience with its well thought indoor and outdoor setting. There's beautiful lighting & wonderful greenery in their garden, making you feel at home. It offers delicious cakes, pastries, an array of bread, sandwiches, coffee, appetizers and a whole lot of Italian cuisine, with their own little twist! Fun Fact: This place looks even more spectacular during the festive season!

LOCATION 24, Cenotaph Rd, Teynampet, Garden Section.
AVERAGE COST FOR TWO INR 800
TIMINGS 11 AM- 11:30 PM
